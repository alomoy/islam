Importance of Quranic Arabic

  • 2:185 الْقُرْآنُ هُدًى لِّلنَّاسِ وَبَيِّنَاتٍ مِّنَ الْهُدَىٰ وَالْفُرْقَانِ ۚ
  • Qur’an was revealed as guidance to man and clear proof of the guidance, and criterion (of falsehood and truth)

  • Book We have sent down to you that you may lead men out of darkness into light

Quran is Easy to Understand

  • 18:1 الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي أَنزَلَ عَلَىٰ عَبْدِهِ الْكِتَابَ وَلَمْ يَجْعَل لَّهُ عِوَجًا ۜ

  • We have made this Qur’an easy to understand; Is there, then, any who will take heed?

Past Tense

Starts with zabar, usually 3 letters

  • Ends with tu: I did
  • خَلَقْتُ
  • Ends with naa: We did
  • Ends with ta () : You did
  • أَنْعَمْتَ, خَلَقْتَ
  • tuma/tum: You (pl) did,
  • Others: فَرَضَ اللَّ, جَعَلَ,
